Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Aragvarord mobile application ("App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or use the App, we may collect:

  • Email address and display name (for account creation via Firebase Authentication)
  • Profile photo (only if you choose to upload one)
  • App usage data — which screens you visit, quiz results, practice session data
  • Device information — device model, OS version, app version (for crash reporting)
  • Crash and error reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain your account and progress across devices
  • To improve the App based on usage patterns (Firebase Analytics)
  • To diagnose and fix crashes and bugs (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • To send you push notifications about your learning progress (only with your permission)

Permissions We Request

  • Camera / Photo Library — only used if you choose to set a profile photo. We do not access your photos otherwise.
  • Push Notifications — optional. You can disable these at any time in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. Data is only shared with Firebase as described above in order to operate the App.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
