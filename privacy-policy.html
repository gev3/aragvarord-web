Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026

Aragvarord does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device only.

What We Store — and Where

The app saves the following data locally on your device using your device's storage. This data is never sent to any server.

  • Profile — first name, last name, and profile photo (optional). Stored only on your device.
  • Quiz progress — which questions you answered, how many times, and whether correctly. Stored only on your device.
  • Driving simulation records — your best scores for crossroad, parking, and highway simulations. Stored only on your device.
  • App preferences — language selection, theme (dark/light), tutorial completion. Stored only on your device.

Permissions We Request

  • Camera — only if you choose to take a photo for your profile picture. We do not access the camera for any other purpose.
  • Photo Library — only if you choose to pick a photo from your library for your profile picture.

You can decline both permissions — the app works fully without them. Profile picture is optional.

Third-Party Services

Aragvarord does not use any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or cloud services. No data is sent to third parties.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete all locally stored data at any time from the Profile screen using the reset options, or by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever add features that change how data is handled, we will update this page and the effective date above.
